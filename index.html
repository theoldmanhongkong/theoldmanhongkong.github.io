Welcome to
The Old Man

Every drink has a story to tell.

It's no secret that booze was Hemingway's life-blood, for him and his fictional cohorts. He was a sophisticated drinker, a lover of fine spirits, and a true connoisseur who sought out the best bars from Paris to Pamplona. The Old Man honors Hemingway by taking inspiration from his novels, history, and folklore, and by using novel ingredients and culinary accoutrements – thereby paying tribute to the spirits and cocktails he loved.
